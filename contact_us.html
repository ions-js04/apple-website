FAQs

How do I track my orders?

Once your order has been placed, you will receive an acknowledgement email to confirm with the details of your order such as shipping and delivery fees for every item include. You will be notified of the order's estimated delivery date and its tracking number. Once your ordered items are ready for shipping, you will be notified via a notification email.

How long should I expect my orders to arrive after purchase?

Once your order has been handed to the carrier and is ready for shipping, you will be notified via an email of the shipment details containing the estimated delivery date, this will be determined by the distance from the warehouse to the given address. Delivery may take up to 3 working days after the shipping of the order.

More info

What should I do if the delivery has arrived when I'm not at home?

Every recevied delivery will require a signature, if our carrier noticed that no one is at home, they will leave a card with a number so that you can contact them a schedule a new delivery date or update your delivery informations.

How much is the delivery fee?

Delivery fees are paid by Apple Store. So it would be free of charge.

What should I do if my delivery is damaged or missing?

If anything has happened to your delivery upon receival or you suspect that it has went missing due to overdue delivery, kindly contact us and we will notify the respective carrier to resolve the issue as quickly as possible.

What is Apple ID?

Apple ID is your apple account. The settings saved on your Apple Device is associated with your Apple ID, the same settings will be applied too if the same Apple ID is logged on different Apple Devices.

What are the benefits of an Apple ID?

By having your own Apple ID, you get to personalize and customize your experience while using Apple Devices and Services such as our Apple Online Store. You can check your account status, apple gift card balance, place online orders and many more.

How to reset my Apple ID password?

You can recover or reset your password easily using our iForgot tool.

Can I have more than one Apple ID?

Yes, although it is not recommended to do so as you would not have a centralize experience. For example, you cannot view your full Apple Store order history in one place.
