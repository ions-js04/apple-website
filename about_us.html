About Apple

Welcome to Apple, Apple is a technology company which founded on April 1, 1976 in California, United States. We are mainly selling different kind of products such as mobile devices (iPhone), tablets (iPad), Macbook, airpod, apple tv and apple watch to customers from all over the world. We also have more than 500 retail locations of our commpany all over the world. We hope that all of our customers will enjoy our products as much as we offering to you.

2011 - 2020

On August 2011, Steve Jobs resigned from position CEO with Tim Cook taking place and iPhone 5 series released. In 2014, the first new product range, Apple watch has been announced. In 2019, iPhone 11 series were released.

2007 - 2011

The first version of iPhone became publicly available in 2007. iPhone 4 introduced in 2010 and Apple's stock market value overtook Microsoft's after resurgence.

1997 - 2007

Steve Jobs returned and bacame CEO of Apple from January 2000 to August 2011. In 2001 , Apple announced the opening of a line of Apple retail stores, to be located throughout the major U.S. markets and iPod has introduced.

1985 - 1997
